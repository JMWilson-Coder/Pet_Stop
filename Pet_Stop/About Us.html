Our Story

Alice Watson developed a love for all creatures as she grew up on her grandmohter's ranch in west Texas. She has made it her mision to provide top quality items for the best price for all pet guardians, no matter which type of critter they love.

Currently Alice and her partner are guardiants to a menagerie of animals including six barn cats, three livestock guaridan dogs, two horses, five goats, a dozen chickens, and a rooster.

Since Alice, opened the first Pet Stop in 1997, we have expanded our services from a dog grooming to meeting a verity of needs for both pets and thier parents. We'd love to hear from you so please connect with us on any of our social media platforms, via email, over the phone or in person.
