Dog Grooming

Our expert groomer know all of the best tequinces and tricks to make your pooch look their best! We have several grooming packages or you can speak to a groomer to customize a package that fits your dog's unique needs and your budget.

Cat Grooming

That's right, cats need grooming too! While cats may not need grooming as often as dogs it's just as important to meet your cat's needs. As cats age, it often becomes difficult for them to keep up with grooming habbits, which means that a cat groomer is needed. It's best to get fluffy used to being groomed and handled as a kitten so they become comfortable with the process. For more infomation contact our expert cat groomers on how best to serve your kitty.


Pet Hotel

Our luxurious pet hotels have everything your pooch or kitty could need at thier home away from home. We have a range of kennel sizes for both cats and dogs. The basic packages include toys, a cot style pet bed, and food and water bowls. Pet guardians are welcomed to bring items from the pet's home, such as toys and bedding. Our premium suits include a 20 inch TV that our TLC team will set to your pet's favorite channel.

Doggie Day Care

When your pooch doesn't need an extended stay, they can enjoy the day with all of their doggie friends! You can opt for personal doggie play times with our TLC staff or for play dates in our inside yard area with other friendly dogs.

Dog Training Academy

Getting a new puppy? Have an old timer who needs to learn new tricks? Our professional dog trainers work with dogs at every stage from puppy kindergarden to the AKC's Cainne Good Citizen class. In additon to three levels of obediance training we offer agility class for atheletically inclined pup.
